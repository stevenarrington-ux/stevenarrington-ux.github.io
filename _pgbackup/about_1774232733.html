About

Hello! I'm Steve.

I've been leading teams and designing simple solutions for complex industries for over 20 years.

I lead teams designing web, mobile, and platform experiences in regulated, data-intensive environments, partnering closely with Product and Engineering to drive clarity, quality, and business impact.

My focus is on building strong teams, shaping sound design strategy, and delivering systems that scale.

I love to laugh—my dad jokes are pretty solid proof—and I look for levity and fun in all I do. When I'm not working, you'll find me outside: roaming trails, kayaking, or just trying something new.
