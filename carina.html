Summary

Carina is the design system that helped save Cvent. At a critical moment for the company — weathering COVID — I coordinated the efforts of 11 designers to build and maintain a cohesive system. The governance model was built to move fast without breaking consistency, enabling the team to ship quality work at scale.

The result: Cvent earned $100M in a single year, with design playing a central role in the product's turnaround.
